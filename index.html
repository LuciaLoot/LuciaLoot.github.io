About

The unclassifiable Lucia Loot is quite a strange musician. Sometimes drawing from the roots of drum & bass, dubstep and bass music, sometimes from the robotic glitches of hyperpop, she knows how to make us both dance and cry. Inspired by the productions of Sophie, the glitches of Skrillex, the ambient dubstep of Burial, the breakbeats of Chase & Status, and the nightcore vocals of 100 Gecs, the transfeminine producer leads us in all directions.

In her debut EP FRUITY LEMONADE (2022), she pays homage to hyperpop and house, the music that helped her survive lockdown. She came back strong with her debut album AVANT ENCORE (2023), the melancholic anthem of a sad android proud of her trans and queer identity. She is planning on releasing several remixes of her latest project in 2024, as well as new tracks soon to be heard in Parisian clubs where the promising DJ will get your feet tapping.

Being the lead singer and guitarist of the Hummingbirds from Saturdays, and lead guitarist of Singing Loudly About Yoland, she is both a producer and a multi-instrumentalist, and has had the opportunity to work on numerous projects, both live and in the studio.

She has worked with :

  • Hummingbirds from Saturdays: musician, singer-songwriter, producer;
  • Singing Loudly About Yoland: guitarist, producer;
  • Elvin Reels: producer, mixing & mastering engineer.
