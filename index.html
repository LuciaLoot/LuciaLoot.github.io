WHO.

Lucia Loot is a queer and transfeminine French-Dutch multi-instrumentalist, producer, lyricist, poet and artist. She is the co-founder, singer and producer of the alternative rock band 'Hummingbirds from Saturdays'. Her lyrics and poetry display an introspective melancholy and a touching light-heartedness, as she explores mental health, gender dysphoria, loneliness and solitude, and feelings of revolt. She is currently working on the recording and production of her upcoming hyperpop project, 'AVANT ENCORE'. President of Big Piaf Music, she also participates in the promotion and development of other musical projects.
