ABOUT

Self-taught in Ableton Live and all kinds of music, Lucia Loot is a year-old transfeminine electronic music producer and singer based in Paris. Following her debut solo EP 'FRUITY LEMONADE', she is currently working on her upcoming album 'AVANT ENCORE' in which she aims to combine her multiple influences : hyperpop, glitch music, dubstep, D&B, UK Garage, and rock. Being a music and technical "swiss knife", she is the president of her own record label 'Big Piaf Music', and singer-songwriter / producer for the indie rock band 'Hummingbirds from Saturdays'.
